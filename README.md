# Udoy5678_PhotoGallery.github.io



-Laravel 5.5
-PHP 7.1
-localhost/phpmyadmin
#Run Command:
-php artisan migrate
-php artisan serve
#Required:
-mailtrap account for email verification
-----------
it's an online photo exhibition system where someone can create his/her account and place their photo with privacy.
--He/she can update/delete their photo.

--Account verification has been performed by email verification using mailtrap.
--he/she can also invite his friends by their mail id.

---His/her friends can download their preferable photo. After downloading the owner get an email with their downloaded photo details.

------------
To see all defined routes and corresponding controller methods use `php artisan route:list` console command
